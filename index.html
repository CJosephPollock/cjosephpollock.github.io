Experiences

Operating Room

I had the opportunity to assist doctors during surgeries in Nuevo Progreso, Guatemala. This is a picture of the incredible staff I worked with in the operating room.

AISCE

I have mentored at risk youth in Oakland, California as a member of the The Academy of Integrated Studies and Community Engagement ("Ace"). In this forward thinking program, we worked to curb gang recruitment and expose our mentees to opportunities available to them.

Mexico Team Building

As part of a team of over 500, I helped in the construction of over 30 houses in Tijuana, Mexico. Part of what made the trip so special was the opportunity to work alongside the family who would eventually live in the house and build relationships with my peers.

Mexico House Building

This photo was taken on the third, and second to last, day of construction. It is truly incredible how quickly we can build a home for a family and what a profound impact that will have on their lives.

Doctors

On the first morning of our medical mission at Hospital de la Familia in Nuevo Progreso, Guatemala. Beside me are several members of the staff that helped perform over 50 surgeries, and remove cataracts restoring the eyesight of over 100 people.
