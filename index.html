What's IS Lorem Ipsum?

Even if you aren't a developer, you are probably familiar with the phrase that starts, "Lorem Ipsum...". I AM a developer, and, like most people, I only knew that it is used as a filler text for an object or a website. The full text reads:

Some websites, such as LipSum.com will generate a filler text for your website for you!
Great! So what is it used for? The Lorem Ipsum text is a jumble of latin words used as a dummy text for your website that has no meaning, but very closely resembles real text. It is used to show the graphic elements of the webpage or document.
