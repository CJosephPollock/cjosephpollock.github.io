Module 4 - Cascading Style Sheets

I will be applying CSS to my HTML structure to change the colors and format of the pages. For example, I plan on making the header one color while the body is a separate color and separating the two with a border-bottom. I will also apply custom fonts, colors, and maybe gradients to differentiate my site and make it aesthetically pleasing and appealing. I plan on making my resume not in a word document, but a physical HTML/CSS page, that has hyperlinks and other interactive elements.

Examples of a few class names and id tags are as follows:

  • For the home page body: id="homePage"
  • For the site header: id="siteHeader"
  • For the site footer: id="siteFooter"
  • For each element on the resume page (i.e. languages I know): class="qualification" (I plan to make these colored buttons that link to a Wikipedia page or another similar explanation.)
  • For the projects, they will most likely follow a similar format, just change pictures and text explanations. They will implement class="projects"
