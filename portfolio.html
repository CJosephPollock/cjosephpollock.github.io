Trakr

Trakr is an iPhone app that I designed that allows a user to download music from YouTube, AOL, and Tumblr for free. It removes the "copy and paste" interface from many YouTube audio downloaders, and creates a convenient, user-friendly interface to download music.

How it works:

  1. Search for music you want

  2. Download music

  3. Export to popular cloud storage options such as: DropBox, Google Drive, and Box.

  4. Enjoy! Optionally upload songs to help the Trakr community
