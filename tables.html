Use of Tables

My website is a personal portfolio, designed to display my work, information about me, and methods with which to contact me.
Tables are fantastic for presenting and organizing data. The content on my site would not benefit from the use of tables, the exception being the resume page.
While I think there is value to building my resume with a table, I have chosen not to. Tables would make it very easy for me to align, edit, and reorganize my resume depending on a given job application. However, first, they are aesthetically unappealing, and second, they limit the functionality I wish to have in the future. After this course, I plan on learning JavaScript and enhancing my resume with the techniques I learn (including drop down sections, etc). Implementing a table would mean undoing that work in the future to implement a more advanced layout method and features on my resume page. Because of this, I have chosen to abstain from implementing tables on my website. However, the "kitchen sink" section of my website will most likely include some table work.
